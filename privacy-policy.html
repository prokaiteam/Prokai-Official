Information We Collect

We collect personal data necessary to provide our Services, such as delivering AI prompt scripts, processing payments, managing referrals, and improving user experience. We adhere to data minimization principles, collecting only what is required. We do not collect or process data related to your interactions with third-party AIs (e.g., ChatGPT, Grok) or your study outcomes, as these are entirely your responsibility.

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account and Profile Information: Email address for sign-in and account creation. You may also upload a profile photo (media files) to customize your profile.
  • Payment Information: When purchasing scripts (Basic, Pro, or God levels), we collect transaction details via Razorpay, including payment method (e.g., UPI, card, bank transfer). We do not store sensitive payment details like card numbers or CVVs; these are handled securely by Razorpay in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
  • Referral and Affiliate Information: Referral codes or links you share, and details of referred users (e.g., their email or transaction IDs) to credit commissions to your in-app wallet.
  • Communication Information: If you request script delivery via email or WhatsApp, we collect the provided contact details. We also collect any information you share in support queries (e.g., via email, WhatsApp, or in-app chatbot).
  • Purchase Details: Exam type, script level, and purchase history to fulfill orders and provide personalized recommendations.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and Usage Data: App usage patterns, device ID, IP address, operating system, and crash reports to improve functionality and analytics. We do not collect location data unless explicitly required (e.g., for geocode-based features, which we do not currently use).
  • App Permissions: We request access to media files solely for uploading profile photos. No other sensitive permissions are required.

c. Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Gateway: Razorpay provides transaction confirmations and status updates. We receive limited data (e.g., transaction ID, amount) with your consent during payment.
  • Affiliates/Referrals: Data about referred installations and purchases to process commissions.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., biometric, health, or genetic data) unless explicitly provided by you for support purposes, and only with your consent.

Clear Instructions for Users:

  • What to Do: Provide accurate data for accounts/payments; consent to permissions only if needed.
  • What Not to Do: Do not provide false information or share sensitive data unnecessarily.
  • Responsibilities: It is your responsibility to ensure data accuracy; ProKai's responsibility is limited to minimal collection for Services.

Google AdMob Policy Compliance

ProKai complies with Google AdMob program policies (which incorporate AdSense policies) for all advertisements displayed in the App, including rewarded ads used to promote or unlock access to our Basic level scripts. We use AdMob to offer users two options for acquiring the Basic script:
(1) purchase with money via Razorpay, or
(2) watch rewarded ads to unlock it for free (no cost, but ads must be viewed completely to receive the script). Pro and God/Advanced level scripts are available only through monetary purchase—no ad-based free access is provided for these tiers

Key Compliance Details:
  • Rewarded Ads for Free Access: Rewarded ads are optional and clearly labeled. Users must actively choose to watch ads to unlock the Basic script, and completion is required for delivery. This aligns with AdMob's rewarded ad guidelines, ensuring user consent and no invalid traffic (e.g., no auto-plays, forced views, or misleading prompts).
  • Ad Placement and User Experience: Ads are placed non-intrusively, without interfering with core App functionality or misleading users (e.g., no ads disguised as App content). We prohibit invalid clicks, such as encouraging accidental taps or offering incentives beyond the script unlock.
  • Prohibited Practices: No ads promote illegal activities, violate intellectual property, or target sensitive categories. We monitor for fraud using AdMob tools and terminate access for violations (as per Terms Point 8 and 12).
  • User Consent and Privacy: Ads require explicit user interaction. Data from ad interactions is handled per our Privacy Policy and DPDPA 2023—no sharing for unrelated purposes. Users can manage ad preferences via device settings.
  • Promotion of Paid Content: Ads may promote all script levels but clearly distinguish free ad-based access (Basic only) from paid tiers. This complies with AdMob's policies on truthful advertising and no deceptive claims.

For more details, refer to Google AdMob policies and AdSense policies.Violations may result in ad suspension or account termination. Contact prokai.team@gmail.com for queries.
