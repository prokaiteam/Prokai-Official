Who We Are

ProKai is an innovative Indian edtech startup dedicated to transforming competitive and academic exam preparation through AI-powered prompt scripts. Founded by a passionate team of visionaries—Raajveer (Founder & CEO), Meemanshoo (Co-Founder & Visionary), and Rituraj (Co-Founder & Product Strategist)—we are registered as a Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprise (MSME) under the Government of India's Udyam Registration, ensuring credibility and compliance with national standards. Our mission is to make high-quality, personalized learning accessible to every student in India, empowering them to "Crack Any Exam with AI" without the need for expensive books, courses, or traditional coaching.

Our Flagship Product

At the heart of ProKai is our flagship product: exam-specific AI prompt scripts available in three levels—Basic (₹250+), Pro (₹350+), and God/Advanced (₹500+)—tailored for exams like SSC, UPSC, CBSE, JEE, NEET, GATE, and more. These scripts, based on 3–10 years of past paper analysis, allow users to paste them into free third-party AI chatbots (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, Claude) to generate unlimited MCQs, topic-wise plans, explanations, and strategies. Delivered instantly via the App, email, or WhatsApp, our scripts support multiple languages (English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali) and work offline through PDFs, making preparation affordable, efficient, and device-agnostic.

Earn While You Learn: Empowering Students Financially

ProKai believes that education should be barrier-free, and no student should face financial hurdles to study effectively. That's why we've designed our platform so you can prepare without spending a dime upfront—through our innovative "Earn While You Learn" referral program.

Here's how it works:

💰 How to Earn

Share your unique referral link or code from the App with friends, family, or on social media. When a referred user downloads the App and purchases a script, you earn up to 10% commission on the sale (net of taxes), credited directly to your in-app wallet. Commissions vary by script level and are verified to prevent abuse (e.g., no self-referrals or spam).

Use Your Earnings Flexibly

Withdraw funds to your bank (subject to minimum thresholds and Indian tax laws) for personal use, or reinvest them in ProKai to buy more scripts—turning your earnings into further learning opportunities. This way, you can fund your entire preparation journey without out-of-pocket expenses.

No Hassle, No Cost Learning

Start for free by referring others and earning commissions to purchase scripts. We handle secure payments via Razorpay, ensuring seamless transactions. Our no-subscription model means lifelong access to your scripts, with no recurring fees.
